Day in the life

of a skater in Austin, TX

Posted by Aziza O'Bryant on April 5, 2020

For myself, I Love staking with friends just as much as a solo session. Both have tought me some valuable life lessons. I wanted to ask Cori and others if they had any insight on skating. The interview questions I asked is listed below.

Question 1- Why do you like skateboarding?

Question 2- What has it done for you?

Question 3- What has skatboarding taught you?

Question 4- Any words of wisdom?

Short documentary on a day in the life of a skater in Austin, TX.



Cast:

  • Cori as the interviewee

    • Ive known Cori for around 8 years, shes seen me grow up bascailly and is always fun to skate with. She often spend her time being a lovley dog mom and being crafty, so cool!

  • Guy

    • In the skate community, you see the same people all the time down here. This Guy is so nice and I always says hey. Unfountally I didnt get his name before he ran off but in my heart I know WE'LL MEET AGAIN!

  • Jacob

    • I went to highschool with this dude, didnt know he skated untill we gradudated. Turns out he just got back into it and now we see each other all the time! Jacob is such a cool dude, down to Earth, and care free. Its always a fun time, THANKS for recording!

  • Bryan

    • Bryan is a little young but he sure does have a heart of steel. This guy is the ULTIMATE hype man and can pop an ollie almost as tall as he is. I can see him progressing very far in this sport and being young he has plenty of time. THANKS for recording!
