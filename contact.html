Figuring out your stance

Have you ever slid with socks on on a hardwood floor? Yes? Then the foot you have forward naturally would be the foot in the front of the skateboard. Take a running start and pretend like you're gonna slide, don't be scared, really put that hip into it :) You'll see what side feels more natural for you to leave with.

Falling the right way

The number one the deterrent of skateboarding is the fear of falling. Unfourtnatlt, that is just a part of skateboarding, it's inevitable. What you can change is HOW you fall. There is a correct way and a way that can get you injured quick. Here are some steps to help you fall without hurting yourself too much.
1.) Roll it off. Your body when falling will have to go from a fast moving position to a stopped position. To combat physics you can roll off the energy making it less painful to fall flat in 1 spot.
2.) Tuck your extremities. When rolling you want to tuck an bend your body. Try not to haveyour arms stright when catching yourself, that can break it. Instead have a slight bend to allivate some tention and pressure. Same goes for neck and legs try to tuck and roll like a rollypolly :)
3.) Have fun
